## Newsletter Structure

Customize based on your format. Example:

```markdown
# [Issue Title]

## Opening
[Personal anecdote or hook - 100-200 words]

## Main Section
[Core content - 500-800 words]

## Quick Hits
- Link 1: [One-line commentary]
- Link 2: [One-line commentary]

## Closing
[Call to action or forward-looking statement]
```

## Subject Line Rules

Use `hook-and-headline-writing` for every subject line.

Test against:
- [ ] Would I open this in a crowded inbox?
- [ ] Does it promise specific value?
- [ ] Is it under 50 characters?
- [ ] Does it avoid clickbait?
