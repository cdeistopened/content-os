# Email Templates Reference

Detailed templates for common email scenarios.

---

## Welcome Email - Community/Movement

**Use when:** Someone joins a community, movement, or organization

```
Subject: Welcome to [Group Name] + [personal reference]

Dear [Name],

Thank you for reaching out to [Group]! I'm [Your Name], [your role].

[Echo their interest/message back - show you read it. E.g., "Your desire for [what they mentioned] resonates with what we're building here."]

**Good news:** [Relevant connection - nearby members, upcoming event, useful resource]

**Here's how to get connected:**

1. [Immediate action with link - join group chat]
2. [What you'll do for them - add to list, make introductions]
3. [Optional next step - virtual meeting, event, resource]

If you'd like to chat by phone to learn more, I'm happy to connect. Just let me know what works for you.

God bless,
[Name]
[Phone]
[Email]
```

## Welcome Email - Professional/Business

**Use when:** New client, customer, or professional contact

```
Subject: Welcome aboard + next steps

Hi [Name],

Great to have you with us.

[One sentence acknowledging what they signed up for/purchased]

**Here's what happens next:**
1. [First thing]
2. [Second thing]
3. [Third thing - optional]

Questions? Reply here or grab time: [calendar link]

[Name]
```

---

## Follow-Up - After No Response (Gentle)

**Use when:** First follow-up after silence

```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [Name],

Wanted to float this back up - [brief restatement of ask].

[Optional: new piece of value - "I also found this resource that might be useful..."]

No rush if now's not the right time.

[Name]
```

---

## Follow-Up - After No Response (Final)

**Use when:** Last attempt before closing loop

```
Subject: Closing the loop on [topic]

Hi [Name],

I'll assume the timing isn't right on [topic].

If things change, I'm here. Otherwise, no need to reply.

[Name]
```

---

## Introduction Email - Connecting Two People

**Use when:** Introducing contacts to each other

```
Subject: Intro: [Name 1] <> [Name 2]

[Name 1], meet [Name 2]. [Name 2], [Name 1].

[Name 1] - [One sentence on why they'd want to know Name 2]

[Name 2] - [One sentence on why they'd want to know Name 1]

I'll let you two take it from here.

[Name]
```

---

## Thank You - After Meeting/Call

**Use when:** Following up after a conversation

```
Subject: Great connecting today

Hi [Name],

Thanks for the conversation today.

[One specific thing you appreciated or learned]

[If applicable: Here's [thing you promised to send]]

[If applicable: I'll [action you committed to] by [timeframe]]

Talk soon,
[Name]
```

---

## Ask for Introduction/Referral

**Use when:** Requesting an intro to someone in their network

```
Subject: Quick ask - intro to [Person/Company]?

Hi [Name],

Hope you're well.

I noticed you're connected to [Person] at [Company]. I'm [brief context on why you want to connect - 1 sentence].

Would you be open to making an intro? Happy to draft something you can forward.

Either way, no pressure.

[Name]
```

---

## Event Announcement

**Use when:** Promoting an upcoming event

```
Subject: [Group] - [Event Name] on [Date]

Dear Friends,

[Opening - connect to season or recent news]

**[Event Name]**
[Day, Date] at [Time]
[Location or "Virtual via Zoom"]

[2-3 sentences: What we'll do, why it matters]

[Practical details - what to bring, what to expect]

**RSVP here:** [Link]

Questions? Reply or call me at [phone].

[Closing],
[Name]
```

---

## Re-Engagement - Dormant Contact

**Use when:** Reaching out to someone who's gone quiet

```
Subject: Been a while + [relevant update]

Hi [Name],

It's been a minute since we connected.

[Brief update on what's new with you/the group]

[Gentle invitation - "Would love to catch up" or "Let me know if you want to grab coffee"]

Hope all is well on your end.

[Name]
```

---

## Apology/Correction

**Use when:** You made a mistake and need to address it

```
Subject: My mistake on [topic]

Hi [Name],

I got [specific thing] wrong in my last email. Here's the correction:

[Correct information]

Sorry for any confusion.

[Name]
```

---

## Declining a Request (Gracefully)

**Use when:** You need to say no

```
Subject: Re: [Their request]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for thinking of me for [request].

I'm not able to take this on right now - [brief, honest reason if appropriate].

[Optional: Alternative - "You might try..." or "I'd suggest reaching out to..."]

Appreciate you reaching out.

[Name]
```

## Subject Line Formulas

### Curiosity + Specificity
- "Question about [specific thing]"
- "Idea for [their project/company]"
- "[Mutual connection] suggested I reach out"

### Value-Forward
- "[Resource] you might find useful"
- "Quick win for [their goal]"
- "Thought of you when I saw this"

### Direct Ask
- "15 minutes this week?"
- "Quick favor"
- "Introduction request"

### Updates
- "[Group] Update - [Topic 1] & [Topic 2]"
- "News from [Group]: [Key update]"
- "[Month] Update + Upcoming Events"

---

## Phrases to Steal

### Warm Openers
- "Thank you for reaching out!"
- "Great to hear from you."
- "This is exactly what I was hoping to hear."
- "Your message made my day."

### Transition Phrases
- "Good news:"
- "Here's what I can do:"
- "Quick update:"
- "One thing to note:"

### Soft Closes
- "No rush on this."
- "Happy to chat more if useful."
- "Let me know what questions come up."
- "I'm around if you want to talk through it."

### Warm Closings
- "Talk soon,"
- "Looking forward to it,"
- "Cheers,"
- "God bless," (faith contexts)
- "In Christ," (Catholic contexts)

---

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

### The Hedge Pile
❌ "I was just wondering if you might possibly have a moment to perhaps consider..."
✅ "Quick question:"

### The Buried Ask
❌ [Three paragraphs of context] ... "Anyway, would you be open to a call?"
✅ "Would you be open to a 15-minute call? Here's why: [one sentence]"

### The Non-Specific Subject
❌ "Quick question"
❌ "Following up"
❌ "Checking in"
✅ "Question about the Fresno event"
✅ "Following up on the intro to [Name]"

### The Premature Ask
❌ Cold email asking for 30-minute call
✅ Cold email asking for 2-minute email reply, then escalate to call
