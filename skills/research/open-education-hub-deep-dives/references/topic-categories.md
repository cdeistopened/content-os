## Content Velocity Notes

From SEO call:
- Pedagogical deep dives: More effort, fewer pieces (2,000-4,000 words)
- Grade-level guides: Can be programmatic/condensed (1,000-1,500 words)
- State guides: Template-based, update as needed
- Tool pages: Need verified reviews to differentiate

## Update Frequency Notes
