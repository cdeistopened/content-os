## 16. The "Things They Won't Teach You" Post


### Description

This post works well because everyone can relate to some kind of experience that didn't properly prepare them for reality. Revealing "hidden" or unconventional wisdom positions you as an insider sharing valuable secrets.

### Example

```
12 Things School Won't Teach You:
1. How to ask for raise.
2. How to cold call.
3. How to sell.
4. How to fire a person.
5. How to and when to hire.
6. How to invest your $.
7. How to stay mentally healthy.
8. How to interview and pick great candidates.
9. How to prospect for new clients without being a douche.
10. How to be grateful.
11. How to truly listen.
12.?

I need your help. ...what's number 12?
Comment it below!

```

### Template

```
{{number}} things {{XXX}} won't teach you

1.{{XXX}}
2.{{XXX}}
3.{{XXX}}
4. {{XXX}}

Any ideas for what number {{XXX}} should be??

```

### How It Works

- Challenges conventional education or training by highlighting its gaps
- Creates a list of practical, real-world skills that most people need
- Positions you as someone with insider knowledge
- Invites participation by asking for suggestions for the final item
- Appeals to people's desire for practical, applicable knowledge

### Example

"Any reason you wouldn't make a purchase from us today?"

```

```

### How It Works

- Offers a better alternative solution
- Keeps it simple and focused on one specific tip
- Invites others to share their own tips, creating a resource in the comments

## Framework 14: The "Three Types of X" Classification


### Description

Creating simple classification systems helps people understand complex topics while positioning you as someone who brings clarity to confusion.

### Example

```
There are three types of professional networkers:

1. THE COLLECTOR: Focuses on quantity, connects with everyone, rarely follows up, measures success by connection count.

2. THE STRATEGIST: Targets specific people, researches before connecting, builds relationships with purpose, measures value not volume.

3. THE COMMUNITY BUILDER: Creates spaces for like-minded professionals, facilitates connections between others, focuses on group value.

Which type are you? I'm a mix of 2 and 3.

```

### Template

```
There are [number] types of [profession/skill/activity]:

1. THE [TYPE ONE]: [Description of characteristics, approaches, and mindset]

2. THE [TYPE TWO]: [Description of characteristics, approaches, and mindset]

3. THE [TYPE THREE]: [Description of characteristics, approaches, and mindset]

Which type are you? Comment below!

```

### How It Works

- Simplifies complex topics through clear categorization
- Creates immediate recognition as people identify their type
- Positions you as someone who brings structure to complexity
- Often generates discussion as people self-identify
- Creates framework for understanding diverse approaches
